Methodology: nominal annual savings are modelled as offset energy (generation × self-consumption, capped at usage) × the escalating tariff, with generation reduced yearly by panel degradation. IRR and NPV are computed on the resulting 25-year cash flow starting with the upfront system cost. LCOE divides discounted lifetime cost (system cost + O&M) by discounted lifetime generation. This is a planning estimate, not a quote — actual results depend on installer pricing, roof orientation, shading, and future tariff decisions.
